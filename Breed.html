Breed Robotics

I am currently working as a software developer for a robotic fish that holds the World Record of “Fastest 50 m swim by a Robotic Fish” with a time of 22.92 seconds. I have helped to successfully establish a robust wireless remote-control communication system over power, turning and rolling commands. Now, I am working to enable robotic vison for automatic detection of obstacles.









Technical Details

  • Arduino to develop software for communication system, enabling power, turning and rolling commands
  • "Check-sum" protocol developed using Arduino at recieving end of signal to filer out trash signals, boosting accuracy
  • Raspberry pi for enabling robotic vision
